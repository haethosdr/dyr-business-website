ABOUT US

The South Bay’s favorite family-owned and operated window and gutter cleaning business.

Rogers Windows and Gutters was started to provide the people and businesses of the South Bay area quality service with local character.

In 2017, we started and still continue, as a father and son team.

We aim to give our costumers professional service with a small family-business personality. This manifests as regular customers built up over time through excellent service, loyal relationships and personal concern. Over the years we have been blessed with serving a unique range of clients. Today, we do work for home owners, renters, small and medium size local businesses, corporate offices, apartments complexes and property management and real estate companies.

Professionalism is one of our highest values. We take good care to use the best quality equipment while treating your space as if it were our own.
